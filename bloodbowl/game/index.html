Kickoff Event

Roll 2D6 on the Kickoff Event table. Results are read as a sum — but doubles matter for some events.

Weather

Roll 2D6 at the start of each half to determine the weather conditions for that drive.

Prayers to Nuffle

If a team has fewer Players on the Pitch than the opposition at kick-off, that team may pray to Nuffle for a blessing. Roll a D16 — there are 16 possible blessings.
